# Email Notifications


Link your email to receive updates when things happen with your invoices.

## Setting Up

1. [Connect your wallet](../getting-started.md)
2. On the connected screen, click **Link Email for Notifications**
3. Enter your email and verify with the code sent to your inbox

Or sign in with email directly — notifications are enabled automatically.

<!-- screenshot: link-email.png — the link email button on the connected wallet screen -->

## What You'll Receive

| Event | Who gets notified | Subject |
|---|---|---|
| Invoice created | Business | "Invoice Created" |
| Factoring accepted | Business | "Factoring Accepted" |
| Debtor payment received | Factor | "Payment Received" |
| Recourse initiated | Business | "Recourse Initiated" |

Emails include the invoice hash, amounts, and relevant addresses so you can quickly identify which deal they refer to.

## Privacy

- Your email is handled by Privy (login) and Resend (delivery)
- We don't sell or share your email
- Emails are transactional only — no marketing
- You can unlink your email at any time from Settings

## If You Don't Want Notifications

Simply don't link an email. The app works the same way — you just won't get email updates. Check your dashboard instead.
